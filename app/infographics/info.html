Virtual ICU

An AI-Powered Revolution in Critical Care Monitoring

The Critical Challenge in Modern ICUs

Intensive Care Units are data-rich environments, but this data overload creates significant challenges. Clinicians face a constant barrage of information, leading to alarm fatigue, delayed interventions, and a critical gap between data availability and effective, trusted application.

1.5%

Increase in mortality risk for each hour of delay in ICU admission for sepsis patients.

Data Overload

Providers are overwhelmed, making it difficult to identify critical patient information quickly.

Alarm Fatigue

High rates of false alarms desensitize staff, leading to missed critical alerts.

The Virtual ICU Solution: A Proactive System of Rescue

Virtual ICU transforms the reactive "failure to rescue" model into a proactive system. By integrating AI with validated clinical scores, it provides real-time risk assessments and actionable alerts, turning data into life-saving insights.
